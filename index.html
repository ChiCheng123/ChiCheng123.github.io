About Me

I am a Postdoctor in National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academic of Sciences. I received the Ph.D degree in 2020 from Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My research interest includes machine learning and pattern recognition, with a focus on object detection, face detection and pedestrian detection.
